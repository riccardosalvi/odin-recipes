Lasagna Recipe

Description

This hearty lasagna recipe is a comforting, flavor-packed dish layered with rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and perfectly cooked pasta sheets. The base starts with a savory blend of ground beef and Italian sausage, simmered in a robust tomato sauce seasoned with garlic, onions, basil, and oregano. A generous layer of velvety béchamel or ricotta adds creaminess between the noodles, while a blend of mozzarella, Parmesan, and provolone melts into a bubbly, golden crust on top.

Each bite offers a satisfying balance of textures and flavors, from the tender pasta to the savory sauce and gooey cheese. Whether you're preparing it for a family dinner, meal prep, or a special gathering, this lasagna holds its structure well and tastes even better the next day. It's a classic comfort food that brings warmth to the table and leaves everyone asking for seconds.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Garlic
  • Salt & pepper

Steps

  1. Cook the noodles – Boil lasagna noodles until tender, then drain and set aside.
  2. Cook the meat – In a pan, cook ground beef with minced garlic, salt, and pepper until browned.
  3. Add sauce – Pour in tomato sauce and let it simmer for 10–15 minutes.
  4. Mix ricotta – In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese with a pinch of salt.
  5. Layer it up – In a baking dish:
    • Spread some sauce on the bottom
    • Add a layer of noodles
    • Spread ricotta
    • Sprinkle mozzarella and Parmesan
    • Repeat layers (sauce, noodles, cheese)
  6. Top and bake – Finish with sauce and plenty of cheese on top. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 30–40 minutes until bubbly and golden.
  7. Let it rest – Cool for 10–15 minutes before slicing.
